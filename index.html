CFO Leadership Awards

The flag bearers of modern corporate world and business strategy influencers, CFOs have emerged as key architects of the new business ripple. The CFO Leadership Awards salute remarkable finance pioneers in India for their ongoing commitment towards excellence, fostering culture of innovation and taking command over business decision for the welfare of an organization.

CFO Leadership Awards is a premier executive program like no other, which celebrates vision, uphold the glory and recognize great leadership. Finalists and winners are selected through an independent peer review process, led by Finance veterans.
